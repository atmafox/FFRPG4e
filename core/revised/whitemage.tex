\begin{jobdesc}[name=pjob-whitemage]
    A magician skilled in healing and protection magic, master of the beneficial magical energies. Its main stats are Water and Fire, with Earth as tertiary stat. Your Spells are primarily defensive in nature, giving you few tools to deal directly with your enemies. The list of Spells and their description is in the Spells section, starting at page 67. \pc

    \textbf{Representatives}: Porom (FFIV), Rosa Farrell (FFIV), White Mage Job (FFI, FFIII, FFV, FFX-2, FFXI, FFT, FFTA) \pc

    \jobstats[hpa=3x,hpb=4x,hpc=5x,hpd=6x,mpa=3x,mpc=4x,armor=Light,weapons=Claws / Gloves \\ Staves \\ Wands]
\end{jobdesc}

\begin{ffminipage}
{\centering \textbf{Abilities}\par }

\tability{Arcane Devotion}: Core Ability acquired at level 1. You can equip \tequip{light armor} and the following weapons: \tequip{Claws / Gloves}; \tequip{Staves}; \tequip{Wands}. You gain the \tspellgroup{Healing} Spell group. Your HP bonus is 3 times your level, the multiplier increasing by 1 at levels 15, 30 and 60. Your MP bonus is 3 times your level, the multiplier increasing by 1 at level 30. \pc

Whenever you cast an \tspellgroup{Elemental}, \tspellgroup{Armor}, \tspellgroup{Shield}, or \tspellgroup{Images} spell, you may do it offensively. If you do, increase the Spell's difficulty to 70. Instead of granting a status effect, an offensively cast spell removes the status effect from the target. An offensive \tspell{Shell} Spell, for example, removes the \tstatus{Shell} status from the target. This Ability can't remove conditions granted by Auto - Status or SOS - Status. \\
\end{jobspec}
